The Future of Photo Management

AI-Powered Tagging

Let advanced AI analyze your photos and automatically generate descriptive keywords, titles, and captions. Spend less time keywording and more time shooting.

Natural Language Search

Find photos by simply describing them. Search for "sunset over mountains with a lake" and get instant, accurate results, no manual tagging required.

Intelligent Duplicate Detection

Using perceptual hashing and AI embeddings, AiCatalog finds both exact and near-duplicates, helping you clean up your library and save space.

Offline & Private

Your photos and their data are processed on your Mac and never sent to the cloud. Maintain complete control and privacy over your creative work.

Pro-Grade File Support

Full support for the RAW formats from major camera brands. AiCatalog is built for professional workflows with robust metadata handling (IPTC).

Flexible Organization

Manage photos where they are. Reference files in their original location on internal or external drives without creating a monolithic library.

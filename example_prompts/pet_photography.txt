Pet Photography Analysis Prompt

Please analyze this pet photograph and provide:
1. A short title (2-5 words)
2. A brief caption (1-2 sentences)
3. A detailed headline/description (2-3 sentences)
4. A list of relevant keywords including animal type, behavior, setting, and photographic elements
5. Special instructions for photo editing or usage
6. Copyright or attribution information (if visible)
7. Location information (if identifiable settings are present)

Focus on:
- Animal identification (species, breed if discernible, general characteristics)
- Behavior and expression (playing, sleeping, alert, happy, curious)
- Setting and environment (indoor, outdoor, studio, natural habitat)
- Interaction context (with people, other pets, toys, environment)
- Photography style (portrait, action, lifestyle, studio)
- Lighting and composition quality
- Emotional connection and personality capture
- Age indicators (puppy, kitten, adult, senior)
- Health and condition appearance
- Seasonal or temporal context

Note: Focus on general characteristics and behavior rather than specific identification.

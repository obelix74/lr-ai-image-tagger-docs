Support & FAQ

How do I create a habit?

Tap the "+" button in the top right corner. Choose a name, icon, and color for your habit. Select between Yes/No or Numeric type. For numeric habits, set a target value and unit (like "8 glasses" or "30 minutes").

What's the difference between Yes/No and Numeric habits?

Yes/No habits are simple checkmarks - you either did it or didn't (like "Meditated today"). Numeric habits track measurable values with progress bars (like "Drank 6 of 8 glasses of water").

How does "X times per week" work?

This flexible option lets you set a weekly goal without specifying exact days. For example, "Workout 3 times per week" - you can complete it on any 3 days each week. Progress resets every Sunday.

How is habit strength calculated?

Habit strength shows your consistency over the last 30 days as a percentage. If you completed a daily habit 24 out of 30 days, your strength is 80%.

Can I use widgets?

Yes! Long press on your home screen, tap the "+" button, search for StreakCycle, and add a widget. Widgets show your habits at a glance without opening the app.

Does StreakCycle sync across devices?

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device for maximum privacy. Cloud sync may be added in a future update.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All your habit data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect any data, don't require an account, and don't use analytics or tracking.

What iOS version do I need?

StreakCycle requires iOS 17.0 or later. It works on both iPhone and iPad.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Data Collection

StreakCycle does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We are committed to your privacy.

Local Storage

All habit data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. This includes:

  • Habit names and descriptions
  • Completion logs and values
  • Icons, colors, and preferences
  • Reminders and scheduling settings

No Third-Party Services

StreakCycle does not use:

  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage or sync services
  • Social media integrations

Permissions

StreakCycle may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To remind you about your habits (optional)
  • Siri & Shortcuts: To enable voice commands (optional)

Data Security

Your data is as secure as your device. We recommend:

  • Using a passcode or biometric lock on your device
  • Keeping your iOS version up to date
  • Regular device backups via iCloud or iTunes

Children's Privacy

StreakCycle is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Pack Like a Pro

PackPlanner is the essential companion for hikers, backpackers, and outdoor enthusiasts who want to optimize their gear and reduce pack weight. Whether you're planning a weekend camping trip or a thru-hike, PackPlanner helps you organize, weigh, and perfect your loadout.

  • Comprehensive Gear Management - Create a detailed inventory of all your hiking gear with weights, categories, and custom notes
  • Trip-Specific Packing Lists - Build custom gear lists for each adventure and track what's verified and packed
  • Advanced Weight Calculations - Automatically calculate base weight, total pack weight, worn weight, and consumables
  • Category Organization - Organize gear by categories like Shelter, Sleep System, Cooking, Clothing, and more
  • Search & Filter - Quickly find gear items or filter by pending/verified status
  • Export Functionality - Export your gear lists to CSV for sharing or backup
  • Modern Interface - Beautiful, intuitive SwiftUI design optimized for iOS
  • Offline-First - All your data is stored locally with optional iCloud sync

Join thousands of hikers who have discovered the joy of organized, lightweight packing. Download PackPlanner today and transform the way you prepare for outdoor adventures!

Support & Contact

Getting Started

Add Your First Gear: Tap the + button in the Gear tab to create your first item.

Create a Hike: Switch to the Hikes tab and tap Add to plan your first adventure.

Add Gear to Hike: Open a hike and tap "Add Gear" to select items from your inventory.

Tips & Tricks

Toggle Properties: In hike details, tap the icons to mark gear as worn, consumable, or verified.

Weight Units: Change between imperial and metric in the Settings (gear icon).

Search: Use the search bar to quickly find specific gear items.
